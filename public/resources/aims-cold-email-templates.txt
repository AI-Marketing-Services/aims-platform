BEST PRACTICES
=================================================================

- Personalize at least the first two lines. No blast templates.
- Keep every email under 100 words.
- One CTA per email. "Reply yes for the deck" converts better than "book a call."
- Follow up 3x over 10 days before dropping a lead.
- Always include your name and your AIMS referral link in the signature.
